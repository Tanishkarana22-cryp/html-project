The best movies according to Tanishka

My top three movies of all time

Bhool bhulaiyaa

An NRI and his wife decide to stay in his ancestral home, paying no heed to the warnings about ghosts. Soon, inexplicable occurrences cause him to call a psychiatrist to help solve the mystery.

Three idiots

Throughout the story, Rancho is telling his friends that don't chase success. But achieve excellence and success will come to you themselves. But, unfortunately, we only want success and don't focus on excellence because it demands hard work, commitment, and passion

Jab we met

One of the best lessons Jab We Met gave us was that one should never kill the inner child in them, just like Geet.
